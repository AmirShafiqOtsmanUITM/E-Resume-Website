Background Story

I was born and raised in the picturesque town of Teluk Intan, Perak, Malaysia, a place renowned for its historical landmarks and cultural heritage. Growing up in this vibrant community, I developed a deep appreciation for the rich traditions and diverse influences that shape our local culture. My early years were spent exploring the natural beauty of Teluk Intan, from the iconic Leaning Tower to the serene banks of the Perak River.
